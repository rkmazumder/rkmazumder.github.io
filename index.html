Ram K. Mazumder, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral Researcher, Civil, Environmental and Architectural Engineering. University of Kansas.

Welcome! My research focuses on application of probabilistic methods for risk, resilience, and functionality assessment of civil infrastructure systems subjected to natural hazards. I am interested in developing a holistic framework that incorporates the performance of civil infrastructure systems and buildings into community disaster resilience analysis. The overarching goal of my research is to analyze community disaster resilience comprehensively, in collaboration with researchers from various disciplines, to narrow the gaps between physical, economic, and social needs of the community. This website contains information about my academic background and research. Thank you for visiting my website. If you are interested in learning more about my research or interested in future collaboration, please contact me.
